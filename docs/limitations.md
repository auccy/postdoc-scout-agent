# Limitations

Postdoc Scout Agent is an MVP research aid. It is not a replacement for human judgment.

## Current Limitations

- Candidate identity is preliminary and not verified.
- Author disambiguation is conservative and can miss or split real identities.
- Affiliation metadata from scholarly sources may be incomplete or stale.
- The U.S. seed map is curated and requires future automated verification.
- OpenAlex, PubMed, NIH RePORTER, and Semantic Scholar coverage can be incomplete.
- Lab openings are not scraped or verified.
- Rankings are evidence triage, not objective measures of mentorship quality.
- No LLM-based summarization is included yet.
- No Streamlit or web UI is included yet.

## Ethical Use

Use the outputs to guide careful review. Do not use them to make unverified public claims about individuals, infer sensitive attributes, spam researchers, or automate decisions about supervisor suitability.

Before outreach, manually verify:

- identity
- current affiliation
- lab or group page
- recent publications and grants
- opening or contact signals
- fit with your actual research goals
